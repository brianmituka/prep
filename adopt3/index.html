about us

adopt a child was started in 2014 by Brian Mituka and Carole Akoth. it was started to help rehabilitate street children and find them foster parents to enable them lead a normal life like other children. Two years down the line 200 children have been successfuly rehabilitated and have already moved into their new homes. some of them are already enjoying massive success in school.

how it works

After filling up the sign up form and the information you gave us has been confirmed as accurate, we will schedule for an interview where the prospective parents will be assessed further for their ability to take good care of the children.
